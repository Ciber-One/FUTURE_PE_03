# AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

## 📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates how Prompt Engineering can be used to generate SEO-optimized blog content and content clusters for business websites.

The goal was to build a system that creates:
- SEO-friendly blog posts
- Structured content clusters
- Internal linking strategies
- Business-focused content for ranking on search engines

---

## 🏢 Business Details
- **Name:** GrowthX Digital  
- **Location:** Bhopal  
- **Type:** Digital Marketing Agency  
- **Target Audience:** Small businesses and startups  

---

## 🎯 Objective
To create a prompt system that generates:
- Long-form SEO blogs
- Content cluster ideas
- Search intent-based topics
- Readable and helpful content

---

## 🧠 Prompt Engineering Approach

### 1. Role-Based Prompting
The AI is guided to act as an SEO content writer to ensure structured and high-quality outputs.

### 2. SEO Structure
Blogs include:
- Title
- Introduction
- H2 & H3 headings
- Conclusion
- Keywords

### 3. Content Clustering
A pillar topic is supported by multiple related blogs to improve SEO performance.

### 4. Local SEO Optimization
Content is tailored for location-based searches (Bhopal).
